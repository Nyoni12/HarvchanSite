Distribution

Transerv is a leading distributor of branded, guaranteed, and quality automotive spares and accessories in Zimbabwe.

Retail services

Transerv operates retail outlets where customers can purchase automotive spares and accessories. All Across Zimbabwe

Fitment services

Transerv has fitment centers where customers can have their purchased products installed by qualified technicians.

Credit sales

Transerv offers credit facilities to eligible customers, allowing them to purchase products and pay later.Only for Civil Servants

Online shopping

Transerv has an online store where customers can browse and purchase products from the comfort of their homes.

After-sales support

Transerv provides after-sales support to its customers, including warranty claims and technical assistance.

Backup water

Transerv sells and installs water tanks and pumps to help customers store and access water during times of drought or water shortages.

Solar panels

Transerv sells and installs solar panels to help customers generate their own electricity and reduce their reliance on the grid.

Backup power

Transerv sells and installs backup power solutions, such as generators and inverters, to ensure that customers have a reliable source of power in case of outages.
