Senior Finance Consultant

Job Summary

Transerv, a leading provider of innovative transportation solutions, is seeking a highly skilled and experienced Senior Finance Consultant to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will play a critical role in providing strategic financial guidance and support to key business units and senior management. This position requires a strong understanding of financial principles, analytical skills, and the ability to translate complex financial data into actionable insights. The Senior Finance Consultant will be responsible for conducting in-depth financial analysis, developing financial models, preparing presentations, and providing recommendations to optimize business performance and drive sustainable growth.

Description

This role involves developing and maintaining sophisticated financial models, conducting in-depth financial analysis, and providing strategic financial guidance to support key business decisions. Responsibilities encompass financial planning, budgeting, forecasting, performance analysis, risk management, and compliance. This includes leading and managing projects, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and effectively communicating financial insights to senior management and stakeholders.
